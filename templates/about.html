About Sign Language Detection

This is a Flask application for detecting sign language using a camera.

Our Mission: Empowering Communication

Communication is a fundamental human right, yet millions of individuals worldwide face barriers due to hearing or speech impairments. Our mission is to empower individuals with these impairments by providing them with a tool that enables seamless communication through sign language.

How It Works

Using cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and computer vision techniques, our system detects and interprets sign language gestures in real-time. By analyzing the movements and shapes of hand gestures, along with facial expressions and body language, our system translates these gestures into text or spoken language.

Why It Matters

Traditional communication aids for the deaf and dumb community often rely on static symbols or text-based interfaces, limiting the fluidity and expressiveness of communication. Our project seeks to overcome these limitations by harnessing the power of technology to create a more intuitive and dynamic communication experience.

Our Vision: A World Without Communication Barriers

We envision a future where individuals with hearing or speech impairments can communicate effortlessly and inclusively with the world around them. By continuing to innovate and refine our sign language detection system, we strive to contribute to the creation of a more accessible and inclusive society for all.
